# Privacy Statement

**Last updated: June 19, 2026**

Labyrinx ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This statement explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. What We Collect

### Website Visitors

When you visit [labyrinx-dev.github.io](https://labyrinx-dev.github.io), we collect:

- **Anonymous usage analytics** via Google Analytics (GA4). This includes pages visited, time on site, referrer URL, browser type, and approximate geographic region (country/city level). No personally identifiable information is collected.
- **No cookies for tracking purposes.** GA4 uses a privacy-first measurement model that does not rely on third-party cookies.

### Software Downloads

When you download Labyrinx from our GitHub releases page:

- GitHub may collect aggregate download counts. We receive only the count, not personal data about who downloaded.
- **The Labyrinx software itself does not phone home.** No telemetry, no usage tracking, no automatic data transmission.

### License Activation

If you purchase a Pro or Enterprise license:

- Payments are processed by **Lemon Squeezy** (a third-party payment processor). We do not receive or store your credit card details.
- We receive your email address and license tier from Lemon Squeezy solely for the purpose of delivering your license key.
- Your license key is verified locally on your machine using HMAC signatures. No online activation check is performed.

### Support Communications

If you email us at [labyrinx@yahoo.com](mailto:labyrinx@yahoo.com):

- We use your email address only to respond to your inquiry.
- We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Improve the website and documentation | Anonymous GA4 analytics |
| Process purchases and deliver license keys | Email + license tier (via Lemon Squeezy) |
| Respond to support inquiries | Your email address and message content |
| Count software downloads | Aggregate download count from GitHub |

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Retention

- Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months (Google's default).
- Email correspondence is retained for as long as needed to resolve your inquiry.
- Lemon Squeezy retains purchase records per their own retention policy.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Request access to personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data
- File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [labyrinx@yahoo.com](mailto:labyrinx@yahoo.com).

Labyrinx is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
